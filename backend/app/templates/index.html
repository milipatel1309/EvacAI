Step 5 / 7

ML risk prediction

A small Random Forest blends the signals you just gathered — alert count & severity, weather (wind, precipitation, temperature), and nearby resource density — into a Low / Medium / High classification with a 0–100 score, confidence, and the reasons behind it.

Responsible AI disclaimer

Evac-AI is a hackathon demo. It does not replace official emergency instructions. Always follow your local government, the National Weather Service / Environment Canada, the Red Cross, and emergency services. AI-generated content can be incomplete or inaccurate — verify critical actions with authoritative sources before acting.
